SECTION I: DEFINITION
	Adventure is a firsthand experience of something incredible and out of your comfort zone with others.
	Adventures are an ultimate goal for life because of the joy, knowledge, and experience they bring to others and yourself.

SECTION II: REQUIREMENTS
	Pictures are not to be thought of and must be ignored. If they arise during an Adventure, take care of them quickly.
	Adventures require your cellphone, wallet, a notepad, and paper.
	Nothing was found by roaming aimlessly with no purpose.
	Being uneasy of the outcome of a plan or event is good.
	On Adventures, be sure to ask questions.
