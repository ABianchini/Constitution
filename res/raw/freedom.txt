SECTION I: DEFINITION
	Freedom is the right to do whatever you want as long as it does not limit the Freedom of others.

SECTION II: SUPERIOR
	Your Freedom was inherited and you will strive to earn it till the day you die.
	Freedom is the Third most important.
	You are entitled to as much Freedom as you earn, for the natural way of things is for people to slowly surrender it to others.
	Your life is not as expensive as Freedom.

SECTION III: REQUIREMENTS
	You are to respect, encourage, and protect the Freedoms of others.
	Your Freedom must be regularly exercised by both act and word.
	You must ask questions daily.
	You must be willing to fight for Freedom, but only as a Last resort.
